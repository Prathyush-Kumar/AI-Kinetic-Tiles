How the Energy Harvesting Tile Works

Step-by-step explanation of the hybrid energy conversion process

1. Footstep Input

When a person steps on the tile, mechanical pressure and motion are applied to the surface structure.

2. Piezoelectric Conversion

Piezoelectric elements generate electrical charge when subjected to compressive stress from the footstep.

3. Triboelectric Generation

Contact and separation between different materials produces additional electrical energy through the triboelectric effect.

4. Mechanical Amplification

Rack-and-pinion and lead-screw mechanisms convert linear motion into rotational motion, increasing energy extraction efficiency.

5. Electromagnetic Generation

Rotational motion drives a coil-magnet generator producing electrical energy through electromagnetic induction.

6. Energy Storage

The generated energy is rectified and stored in capacitors or rechargeable batteries with over-voltage protection.

7. IoT Monitoring

ESP32 measures voltage and energy parameters and transmits real-time data to a web-based dashboard via Wi-Fi.

8. Smart Optimization

AI models analyze footstep patterns and energy trends to optimize harvesting and predict system performance.
